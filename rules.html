  1. Regular Pieces

    1. Each player starts with 12 regular pieces on each of the black squares of the first three rows on their side.
    2. One player's regular pieces are black. The other player's regular pieces are red.

  2. Kings

    1. No players start with kings.
    2. The color of kings are the same as regular pieces, but they generally look bigger or fancier.
    3. Placing a regular piece on the opposing player's first row turns it into a king
